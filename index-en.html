Snow Report

Hello everyone,

The weather forecast is excellent to come and enjoy the last weekend of operation at Le Massif. Heat, sun and spring conditions will be there to make the most of this beautiful day. With the warm temperatures of the last few days, we offer the Camp-Boule sector, with access to the mid-station of the gondola via La Coulé. The lifts will be in operation at 8:30am.

At the top, we will have BBQ, Hot-Dog and drinks from 11am to 4pm. La Forêt Gourmande du Massif and Ah la Vache with its famous Brezfondu will also be located at the top of the mountain.

Wearing a mask is mandatory in queues, chair lifts and closed public places. Compliance with sanitary measures is essential for our season to last.

We invite you to consult our skier's guide to prepare yourself well before your arrival at the mountain. We update it regularly. Please respect the sanitary measures put in place. Have a good weekend.
